Yachteo™ is a concept solution created with the aim to provide a completely new user experience for anyone looking to use a yacht chartering website. This concept is brought to you by fiftyseven — a design studio that focuses on creating digital design for emotion and overall strategy. Our idea was to give the user that creative, interactive and unique experience. The fully functional product will present our exploration, decision making, and vision of what a modern yacht chartering website should look like.
